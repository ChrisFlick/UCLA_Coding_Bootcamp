Bio

Joel Zimmerman also known as deadmau5 is a dance music producer known for making progressive house. In college Joel was going to school for computer science when he soon discovered he had a knack for making music. While he mostly makes music under deadmau5 he has also produced music under the alias testpilot.

Find out more at his wikipedia page.
